Safety

"Safety" has become one of the most searched keywords since COVID-19 epidemic. COVID-19 can spread between people through respiratory droplets or small particles produced when an infected person coughs, talks, or breathes. Growing evidence shows that droplets can remain suspended in the air and travel distances beyond six feet, according to the CDC.

In this topic, we focus on food traceability and contactless delivery system.

Food Traceability

The growth of food safety in post COVID-19 has increased the importance of traceability, as it indicates quality and safety guarantee. Traceability means the ability to generate a detailed transaction history for a particular product (where it is from), including the physical location, time spent at each location, record of ownership, packaging configurations and environmental storage conditions.
