Information Systems and Travel post COVID-19

The public health emergency of COVID-19 pandemic has caused an unprecedented impact on human lives since 2020. Over five million lives have been officially claimed since its outbreak, according to data from the Johns Hopkins Coronavirus Resource Center (figure 1).

As one of the most vulnerable sectors exposed to the pandemic, tourism has been severely affected. Thanks to information system, we can easily know that the number of tourists is decreasing and tourism is being affected to some extent all over the world (figure 2).
