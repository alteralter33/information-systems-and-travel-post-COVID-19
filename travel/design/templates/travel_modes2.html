The online "cloud tourism" model can not only meet the cultural needs of the public, but also relieve the epidemic pressure and preheat the recovery of tourism.

During the epidemic, hotels, catering, transportation and other modules were forced to shut down, which dealt a heavy blow to the whole tourism industry. In order to survive, scenic spots everywhere need to find a new way out.

Usually, the live broadcast of tourism with high audience participation is mostly the overall tour mode of the scenic spot. The anchor leads users to visit all details online and publicize the scenic spots.

Cooperate with the interaction between the anchor and tourists to improve the audience's consumption intention of the scenic spot.

It is reported that the online live broadcast of Longmen Grottoes has brought a total of 28 million exposure to Longmen Grottoes scenic spot, effectively improving its online promotion efficiency.

Under the lens of senior tour guides and female anchors, netizens visited the magnificent posture of the Millennium ancient Grottoes through live broadcast. The tour guides' vivid explanations attracted 384000 home visitors to watch online at the same time.

During the live broadcast, netizens can ask questions and participate in interaction through the comment area at any time. Through online tourism, people can still intuitively feel the wisdom and skills of the ancients. The live broadcast allows thousands of netizens to enjoy the beautiful scenery of the motherland, release their depressed mood and enjoy rich and colorful cultural life without leaving home.
